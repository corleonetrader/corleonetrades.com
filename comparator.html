What is a prop firm?

TRADE INSTITUTIONAL CAPITAL. KEEP THE PROFIT.

A futures prop firm funds your trading account after you pass a performance evaluation. Instead of risking your own capital, you trade the firm's money and keep a split of the profits. The three numbers that matter most: the True Cost (what you actually pay after discounts), the drawdown limit (how much you can lose before the account fails), and the profit target (what you need to hit to get funded).
