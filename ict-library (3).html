Introduction

Before the vocabulary, before the models, before the mentorships — there is the Market Maker Series. This is where ICT explains the engine behind price: why it moves, who moves it, and what the algorithm is actually doing at every turn. Watch the Forex Series and the Primer Course first, in full. Do not take notes on entry models yet. Your only job here is to understand that price is not random — it is a precision delivery mechanism targeting liquidity. Once that lands, everything else makes sense.

"You're not skipping this. I've seen what happens to people who skip the introduction. Just watch the series." — DP

This is not optional background material. It is the dictionary. You will not understand the mentorships without it. Watch each month fully before moving to the next. Revisit after the mentorships — it hits differently the second time.

The Core Path
Mentorship Series

This is the curriculum. Four years of live teaching, each year building on the previous one. The order is not a suggestion — 2022 establishes the framework that 2023 perfects. Skipping ahead is like reading chapter 10 without chapters 1 through 9: you will think you understand, but you will be missing the reasoning behind every model. Work through each year before starting the next. Take your time with 2023.

"2022 → 2023 → 2024 → 2025. In that order. The 2023 is the good stuff. Like, really good stuff. Don't tell anyone I said that." — DP

Step 01
2022 Mentorship

The starting point. ICT rebuilds the framework from scratch — liquidity, draw on price, time and price theory, PD arrays in context. This year strips away every retail concept and replaces it with how the market actually works. Do not rush it.

The pinnacle. Every concept from 2022 recontextualised, sharpened, taken to a level of precision no other public resource comes close to. ICT teaches live, calling moves before they happen. Psychology
